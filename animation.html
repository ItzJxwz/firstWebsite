"The Ultimate Catastrophe" Animation.

Software: Adobe After Effects.

I decided to do a short film done in after effects based on the safety of driving. I wanted to do this through entertainment, so I tried to do this in a comedic aspect. I used the funny audios from “freesounds.org” . I chose these colors and shapes being that red and black are my two favorite colors, and I used the rectangles to represent the window and the body of the vehicle. The most challenging part of this project was figuring out HOW I was going to animate this. I didn’t know where to start. I had about two drafts prior to this one. In terms of timing, I had to use after effects on the campus, being that my computer wasn’t allowing me to use it at home, so I never got to finish the project on time. But... Here it is!!
